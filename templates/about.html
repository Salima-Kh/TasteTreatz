About Our Web App

Our web app is designed to help users discover new recipes based on their dietary preferences and restrictions. We aim to provide a seamless experience for finding delicious and healthy recipes, making it easier for everyone to explore new dishes and maintain a balanced diet.

Features

Personalized Results

Our web app provides personalized search results based on your selected ingredients, diet labels, health labels, cuisine types, and dish types.

Bookmark Recipes

Save your favorite recipes and access them anytime in your bookmarks. Our bookmarking feature allows you to easily access and manage your favorite recipes.

Caloric Needs Calculator

Calculate your daily caloric needs based on your age, weight, height, activity level, and gender. This feature helps you to maintain a healthy diet by understanding your body's energy requirements.

Our Team

We are a group of passionate developers and food enthusiasts who believe in the power of technology to improve our lives. Our mission is to create a platform that makes it easy for everyone to discover and enjoy delicious, healthy recipes.
